Moon Down

Beautiful looks the moon !

Feeling it's cool

Peping like fool

It hovers, on its own rule

Beautiful, is the moon

dark patch all on

makes night fall on

baby cry stops

rem'ber the tales we cross

It's out there like a spy,

walking beauty on sky

sky above the high

sleepless nigh'

you're shy

by the window, I

Wintry wish

people are red, sun on head

too much when land breath

it comes with cool breeze

a bit of freeze and a little bit sneeze
