EcoBrick & Wellness Fiji

Benifits of Eco-brick Making and Collection

  1. Working for making Eco bricks will give young stars a daily/weekly challenge to be indulged in.
  2. This will not only boost their self-confidence but also will help to maintain environment healthy and free from plastic.
  3. This campaign will give rise to experienced persons who have knowledge and experience to deal with eco bricks, can teach and encourage children in school to make more bricks.
  4. In this way a chain-reaction for building eco bricks can be made easily for better future.

Social and physical Engagements

  • Macking of Ecobricks can be Introduced as a Extracurricular Activity in school/colleges.
  • In this way children as well as adults will get a new interesting way to get them connected to nature as well to people
  • This will give them Encouragement to work further and to Carry on, also it will help to Fight with Stigma, the Fijian people face before getting disgraced.
