For you

Antiques make a place,a Museum

come and make my heart that one.

Like the way,if your name is cake

One piece out of it,I wanna take.

Can't I stop you from being fed

isn't your dress so red.

No need to call a life-guard

Already,I'm drowned in your eye.

Distance

never met,never touched

never thought, it was too far

Every day,every night used to bring

Writing Good morning

Only two mobile screen

it was in between

Realised, distance was nothing

bonding is everything

The day you measured the distance

we really bacame far

Walk a bit

Don't know if it'd fit

but still,

Wanna ask you

by me can you please sit

Even if you pretend;

with me,

Can you walk a bit?

Dear you

A sight of you

is joy to me.

When you smile,

blows cool breeze on me

a sad Face turns Happy

Hearing you,

birds starts chriping

heart skips beating

That's why

A presence of you

is life to me !!!
